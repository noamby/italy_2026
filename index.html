MAY 28 • INTROD

Day 2: Supermarkets & Settling In

🏠 Stay: Introd Airbnb (Aosta Valley)
📍 Google Maps

🛒 Stocking Up (Milan to Aosta)

  • 📍 Esselunga di Gallarate: 15 mins from Malpensa. Massive hypermarket perfect for GF basics, diapers, and week-long supplies. Best if you choose Option A.
  • 📍 Gros Cidac (Aosta): Located right in Aosta town. Incredible local meats, Fontina cheese, and local goods. Best if you choose Option B.
🌡️ 13°C (Hills) 🚗 1h 45m Drive 🥾 2km Walk Stroller OK

👗 Dress Code: T-shirt + leggings. Pack a cozy sweater for the baby & adults for wooded afternoon shade.

🎒 What to take for the day
  • Baby carrier or stroller
  • Light Fleeces/Sweaters
  • Snacks for the drive
  • Tablet for the drive
  • Shoes

Morning: Slow start. Drive to Esselunga di Gallarate for groceries. 1.5-hour drive into Aosta Valley.

Afternoon: Check into Introd. Visit Parc Animalier d'Introd to see ibex, chamois, marmots, owls, and deer on wide stroller-friendly trails.

🍽️ GF Lunch: Lo Tatà (Introd)
Very close to the park. Authentic local food with a friendly atmosphere; they are familiar with dietary restrictions.
🌡️ 21°C (Valley) 🚗 1h 30m Drive 🥾 2.5km Walk Stroller OK

👗 Dress Code: Light layers suitable for city walking.

🎒 What to take for the day
  • Stroller
  • Tablet for the drive
  • Shoes

Morning: Drive to Aosta town. Stock up at Gros Cidac.

Afternoon: Walk Aosta Town Center and visit the Roman Theater. Enjoy a long gelato break in the pedestrian zone.

🍽️ GF Lunch: Trattoria degli Artisti (Aosta)
Located in the center with a good understanding of gluten-free needs. Great for a sit-down meal amidst the ruins.
MAY 29 • COGNE / BARD

Day 3: Nature's Roar

🏠 Stay: Introd Airbnb (Aosta Valley)
📍 Google Maps
🌡️ 11°C (Falls) 🚗 1h Drive 🥾 1.5km Walk Carrier

👗 Dress Code: Base layer t-shirt with fleece & windbreaker. High-traction sneakers. Carrier wind-shield.

🎒 What to take for the day
  • Baby carrier
  • Rain Jackets
  • Snacks for the drive
  • Shoes

Morning: Drive to Cogne. Hike Lillaz Waterfalls. An easy 1.5km loop, the falls are thunderous in May due to snowmelt.

Afternoon: Relax at Sant'Orso Meadows in Cogne. Let the kids use the large playground while you enjoy the glacier views.

🍽️ GF Lunch: Ristorante Lou Ressignon (Cogne)
Excellent and highly rated for gluten-free traditional dishes (or opt for a meadow picnic).
🌡️ 18°C (Fort) 🚗 1h 10m Drive 🥾 1km Walk Carrier

👗 Dress Code: Comfortable indoor walking clothes.

🎒 What to take for the day
  • Baby carrier
  • Light Jackets
  • Shoes

Morning: Drive down the valley to Forte di Bard. Ride the futuristic glass elevators to the top level.

Afternoon: Explore the Museum of the Alps inside the fort (interactive for kids). Stop in the tiny medieval village of Bard for a snack.

🍽️ GF Lunch: La Polveriera (Inside the Fort)
Convenient for families mid-tour. Offers safe allergen handling.
🌡️ 18°C (Valley) 🚗 40m Drive 🥾 2km Walk Carrier

👗 Dress Code: Comfortable outdoor wear for walking with animals, light jacket for the mountain gondola.

🎒 What to take for the day
  • Baby carrier
  • Light Jackets
  • Shoes

Morning: Drive to Alpaca Sapé (Doues). Enjoy a guided 1.5-hour nature walk where the kids get to lead their own alpacas! (Must book via WhatsApp/Instagram in advance).

Afternoon: Drive down to the Aosta-Pila Gondola parking. Ride the cable car 20 mins up to Pila for ice cream and panoramic valley views.

🍽️ GF Lunch: Enjoy a meal at one of the mountain-view cafes in Pila, or grab GF snacks before taking the gondola back down.
MAY 30 • FÉNIS / SARRE

Day 4: Castles & Gardens

🏠 Stay: Introd Airbnb (Aosta Valley)
📍 Google Maps
🌡️ 15°C (Valley) 🚗 30m Drive 🥾 1km Walk Stroller OK

👗 Dress Code: Fleeces or light down jackets. Wrap baby in full fleece suit.

🎒 What to take for the day
  • Baby carrier or stroller
  • Picnic Lunch
  • Shoes

Morning: Explore Sarre Royal Castle and see the famous "Horn Gallery".

Afternoon: Spread a blanket in the Castle Gardens. It's a very flat, safe grassy area for the baby to crawl while you relax.

🍽️ GF Lunch: Picnic in the Gardens
This is a low-key day. Best to have a light lunch back at your Airbnb or a simple picnic in the gardens.
🌡️ 15°C (Valley - Rain) 🚗 50m Drive 🥾 1.5km Walk Carrier

👗 Dress Code: Keep umbrellas in the car. Sturdy, warm shoes.

🎒 What to take for the day
  • Baby carrier
  • Umbrellas
  • Warm baby fleece
  • Shoes

Morning: Visit Fénis Castle. Take the guided tour to see the colorful frescoes and the double defensive walls.

Afternoon: Drive to nearby Nus for a slow lunch, then head back to Introd for a relaxed afternoon indoors or by the local stream.

🍽️ GF Lunch: Ristorante Comtes de Challant (Fénis)
Just a short walk from the castle, very accommodating for children and offers GF pasta/risotto.
MAY 31 • COURMAYEUR

Day 5: High Peaks

🏠 Stay: Introd Airbnb (Aosta Valley)
📍 Google Maps
🌡️ 7°C (Skyway) 🚗 1h Drive 🥾 1km Walk Carrier

👗 Dress Code: Warm winter gear! Puffer jackets, hats, gloves, sunblock, and sunglasses are mandatory.

🎒 What to take for the day
  • Baby carrier
  • Warm baby fleece
  • Gloves
  • Beanies
  • Shoes

Morning: Take the Skyway Monte Bianco to the Pavillon station (2,173m). Visit the "Skyway For Kids" playground.

Afternoon: Explore the Saussurea Alpine Botanical Garden before taking the cable car back down.

🍽️ GF Lunch: Restaurant Bellevue (Pavillon Station)
Buffet-style but high quality; they clearly label allergens. Eating at 2,000m with a view of the peaks is memorable.
🌡️ 22°C (Valley) 🚗 1h 20m Drive 🥾 2km Walk Carrier

👗 Dress Code: Valley drive is warm—t-shirts suffice.

🎒 What to take for the day
  • Baby carrier
  • Snacks for the drive
  • Shoes or sandals

Morning: Take the scenic drive through Val Ferret. Park at the end of the public road.

Afternoon: Take a very easy stroll along the flat valley floor river. Find a safe "beach" of pebbles for the kids to play in the water.

🍽️ GF Lunch: Ristorante Pizzeria L'Etoile (Val Ferret)
Very rustic and welcoming. Hearty mountain stews and polenta that are naturally GF.
🌡️ 5°C (Summit) 🚗 1h 10m Drive 🥾 1km Walk Carrier

👗 Dress Code: Full winter gear for the snowy peak! Puffer jackets, gloves, hats, and high-SPF sunscreen.

🎒 What to take for the day
  • Baby carrier
  • Warm baby fleece
  • Gloves
  • Beanies
  • Shoes

Morning: Drive to Breuil-Cervinia. Park and take the Matterhorn Cable Cars up to 3,480m to play in real summer snow. A thrilling high-altitude experience.

Afternoon: Drive down 5 minutes to Lac Bleu. A tiny, stunning blue lake perfect for a scenic picnic and feeding ducks.

🍽️ GF Lunch: Eat at the peak or bring your own picnic to enjoy at Lac Bleu (as the lake isn't overly touristy).
JUNE 01 • LAGO D'ARPY

Day 6: High Alpine Lakes

🏠 Stay: Introd Airbnb (Aosta Valley)
📍 Google Maps
🌡️ 10°C (Lake) 🚗 1h Drive 🥾 3km Walk Carrier

👗 Dress Code: Active hiking pants, sneakers, layered fleece & windbreaker. Sun hats and sunscreen are essential.

🎒 What to take for the day
  • Baby carrier
  • Picnic Lunch
  • Shoes

Morning: Drive to Colle San Carlo. Hike 45 mins to Lake Arpy. Wide, gentle forest walk.

Afternoon: Spend a long afternoon by the lake. It's a perfect spot for skipping stones. Hike back at a slow pace.

🍽️ GF Lunch: Picnic Mandatory!
There are no restaurants at the lake. Pack some high-quality GF bread and local meats from Gros Cidac.
🌡️ 20°C (Valley) 🚗 50m Drive 🥾 2km Walk Stroller OK

👗 Dress Code: Casual village wear.

🎒 What to take for the day
  • Baby carrier or stroller
  • Light Fleeces/Sweaters
  • Shoes

Morning: Stroll through Pré-Saint-Didier. Visit the local artisanal chocolate shops and bakeries.

Afternoon: Find the village playground with views of the Mont Blanc massif. Have a slow "merenda" (afternoon snack).

🍽️ GF Lunch: Ristorante Pizzeria l'Escale
A cozy spot in the village with a dedicated gluten-free menu.
JUNE 02 • GRESSAN

Day 7: Open Spaces

🏠 Stay: Introd Airbnb (Aosta Valley)
📍 Google Maps
🌡️ 18°C (Valley) 🚗 30m Drive 🥾 2.5km Walk Stroller OK

👗 Dress Code: Have packable waterproof jackets in your backpack. Rain is expected around 15:00.

🎒 What to take for the day
  • Stroller
  • Rain Jackets
  • Picnic Lunch
  • Shoes

Morning: Head to Gressan Recreation Park. Rent a family bike or just walk the paved paths.

Afternoon: Use the large "Les Îles" playground and feed the ducks at the small pond. Very easy logistics for the baby.

🍽️ GF Lunch: Picnic on-site
This day is very active. A packed lunch in the park is much easier with three kids than trying to find a restaurant mid-play.
🌡️ 18°C (Indoor) 🚗 1h 10m Drive 🥾 1km Walk Stroller OK

👗 Dress Code: Casual indoor wear.

🎒 What to take for the day
  • Baby carrier or stroller
  • Shoes

Morning: Sleep in and enjoy a long breakfast in Introd.

Afternoon: Drive up to the Saint-Barthélemy Planetarium to attend a family show.

🍽️ GF Lunch: Osteria del Passet
Small, traditional, and very friendly. Best to call ahead to ensure they have GF options ready.
🌡️ 18°C (Valley) 🚗 1h 20m Drive 🥾 1.5km Walk Stroller OK

👗 Dress Code: Casual activewear. Bring a light jacket as Lago Gover is at a slightly higher elevation.

🎒 What to take for the day
  • Baby carrier or stroller
  • Picnic Lunch
  • Shoes

Morning: Drive to Sunny Ranch Antey for 25-minute pony rides (book in advance). A great pastoral setting for the kids.

Afternoon: Drive further up the valley to Lago Gover. It has a beautiful tiny lake, a massive grass area for frisbee, and a great playground.

🍽️ GF Lunch: Pack a lunch to eat on the grass at Lago Gover, or grab GF toasts at La Pineta nearby if their kitchen is closed.
JUNE 03 • VALNONTEY

Day 8: Slow Alpine Living

🏠 Stay: Introd Airbnb (Aosta Valley)
📍 Google Maps
🌡️ 21°C (Warm sun) 🚗 1h Drive 🥾 3km Walk Stroller OK

👗 Dress Code: Lightweight t-shirts, shorts or hiking trousers, sunglasses, and sun hats.

🎒 What to take for the day
  • Baby carrier or stroller
  • Sun Hats
  • Light Fleeces/Sweaters
  • Shoes

Morning: Take the flat walk towards Valnontey (very easy for carriers).

Afternoon: Relax at a cafe in Cogne Square while the kids play in the nearby park area.

🍽️ GF Lunch: Ristorante Valnontey
Perfect post-walk lunch before heading to the square. Accustomed to hikers with dietary needs.
🌡️ 21°C (Indoor/Caves) 🚗 50m Drive 🥾 1.5km Walk Carrier

👗 Dress Code: Bring a light pullover for the cold cheese caves.

🎒 What to take for the day
  • Baby carrier
  • Light Fleeces/Sweaters
  • Money for local goods
  • Shoes

Morning: Visit a Fontina Caseificio in Saint-Christophe for a tour of the aging caves.

Afternoon: Go to the Maison des Anciens Remèdes in Jovençan—an interactive museum about mountain herbs.

🍽️ GF Lunch: Cook at Airbnb
Since you'll be touring cheese cellars, it's a great day to eat a late lunch back at the Airbnb using local ingredients.
JUNE 04 • LAKE ORTA / MAGGIORE

Day 9: Transfer to the Lake

🏠 Stay: Lake Maggiore Airbnb
📍 Google Maps
🛒 Stocking Up: Aosta to Lake Maggiore
  • 📍 Ipercoop Le Isole (Gravellona Toce): Right on the highway junction entering the lake region. Biggest supermarket in the area. Best for either Option A or B on the drive in.
  • 📍 Carrefour Market (Stresa): Good for smaller, daily top-ups once you arrive at your new base.
🌡️ 23°C (Sunny) 🚗 2h Drive 🥾 1.5km Walk Carrier

👗 Dress Code: Breezy linen, short sleeves. Carry a light hoodie for open boats on the water.

🎒 What to take for the day
  • Baby carrier
  • Light Fleeces/Sweaters
  • Sun Hats
  • Tablet for the drive
  • Shoes

Morning: Drive 1.5 hours to Orta San Giulio. Take the ferry to Isola San Giulio.

Afternoon: Walk the "Way of Silence." Return to the mainland and check into your Lake Maggiore accommodation in the late afternoon.

🍽️ GF Lunch: Ristorante Venus (Orta San Giulio)
Right on the water. Well-versed in GF catering and the view is spectacular.
🌡️ 23°C (Overcast) 🚗 2h Drive 🥾 2km Walk Stroller OK

👗 Dress Code: Casual town wear. Travel umbrellas.

🎒 What to take for the day
  • Stroller
  • Umbrellas
  • Tablet for the drive
  • Shoes

Morning: Drive to Orta San Giulio village. Explore the car-free stone streets.

Afternoon: Have lunch in the main piazza. If raining, visit the Sacro Monte di Orta via a short, easy drive up the hill.

🍽️ GF Lunch: Leon d'Oro
Located in the main square. Solid GF menu and easy to keep an eye on the kids playing in the piazza.
JUNE 05 • STRESA

Day 10: Islands & Safari

🏠 Stay: Lake Maggiore Airbnb
📍 Google Maps
🌡️ 24°C (Lake) 🚗 0m (Ferry) 🥾 2km Walk Stroller OK

👗 Dress Code: Breathable summer wear. Bring a light down vest/jacket for late PM ferry breezes.

🎒 What to take for the day
  • Baby carrier or stroller
  • Light Jackets
  • Shoes

Morning: Take the boat from Stresa to Isola Madre. Explore the botanical gardens and the white peacocks.

Afternoon: Visit the Puppet Museum inside the island's Palace. Take the ferry back and walk the Stresa promenade.

🍽️ GF Lunch: Mamma Mia (Stresa)
Eating on the islands is expensive. Grab a snack there and have proper lunch back in Stresa at Mamma Mia (famous for GF options).
🌡️ 24°C (Park) 🚗 10m Drive 🥾 2.5km Walk Stroller OK

👗 Dress Code: Walk-friendly sneakers or sandals.

🎒 What to take for the day
  • Stroller
  • Picnic Lunch
  • Shoes

Morning: Visit Villa Pallavicino. Spend time in the petting zoo area with goats and deer.

Afternoon: Use the villa's massive playground and enjoy a picnic under the ancient sequoia trees.

🍽️ GF Lunch: Ristorante Pizzeria Lo Stornello
A certified AIC (Celiac Association) restaurant in Stresa.
JUNE 06 • MOTTARONE / CANNOBIO

Day 11: Rollercoasters & Shores

🏠 Stay: Lake Maggiore Airbnb
📍 Google Maps
🌡️ 15°C (Summit) 🚗 10m Drive 🥾 1.5km Walk Carrier

👗 Dress Code: Requires fleece/jacket for the peak breeze.

🎒 What to take for the day
  • Baby carrier
  • Light Jackets
  • Shoes

Morning: Take the cable car to Mottarone. Ride the Alpyland coaster (age 4+ can ride with you).

Afternoon: Visit the Alpinia Botanical Garden at the mid-station for the best lake views before heading down.

🍽️ GF Lunch: Ristorante Casa della Neve
Located near the top. Typical mountain food; they easily accommodate GF requests with polenta-based dishes.
🌡️ 25°C (Beach) 🚗 1h Drive 🥾 2km Walk Stroller OK

👗 Dress Code: Pure beach mode - wear swimwear under light clothes. Bring towels and water shoes.

🎒 What to take for the day
  • Stroller
  • Swimwear & Towels
  • Sandals
  • Sun Hats

Morning: Drive 30 mins north to Cannobio. Stroll the wide, paved lakefront.

Afternoon: Let the kids play at the Lido (beach). The water is shallow and safe for splashing.

🍽️ GF Lunch: Ristorante Pizzeria Sancarlo
Right on the promenade. They offer GF pizza and pasta and have plenty of outdoor seating to watch the lake.
